<h2>User Manual</h2>
<br><img src=fotobot320_227px.jpg><br>

Fotobot is a good solution for time laps photography.<br>
<br>
Below are main features of Fotobot:<br>
<br>
1) connect to Internet via Wi-Fi or MobileData;<br>
<br>
2) stays running (doesn't allow Android to kick out from memory);<br>
<br>
3) works on all devices starting from Android 2.3;<br>
<br>
4) installation package less than 1MB (important for old phones with small amount of internal storage);<br>
<br>
5) can be adjusted for slow-CPU devices (has an option for timing between processes).<br>
<br>
6) detailed reports in email (signal strength (db), battery charge(%), battery temperature (C), free space (MB));<br>
<br>
7) can be controlled via SMS;<br>
<br>
8) camera flash;<br>
<br>
9) internla/external(SD) storage;<br>
<br>
10) offline mode (no emails, just saving photos into storage);<br>
<br>
<h1>Typical workflow (fast start)</h1>
Launch Fotobot and make some important settings<br>
<br>
<u><b>Settings/Behaviour</b></u><br>
<br>
<b>Camera Name</b> (f.e. Sunrise), helps to sort out email in Inbox.<br>
<br>
<b>Storage Type</b><br>
<br>
<b>Pause between time laps frames (sec)</b><br>
Do not use numbers less than 30sec, cause old phones do not have powerfull CPU for image processing.<br>
<br>
<u><b>Settings/Network</b></u><br>
<br>
<b>Network</b><br>
on - photos will be sent to email;<br>
off - photos will be written to SD-card;<br>
<br>
<b>Internet connection</b><br>
Select Wi-Fi, Mobile Data or both<br>
<br>
<b>Fotobots email</b><br>
It sounds strange, but Fotobot has to have it's own email address (use email account with one level authentication (without SMS confirmation)).<br>
<br>
<b>Fotobots password</b><br>
Password for Fotobots email account.<br>
<br>
SMTP server and port are very important too. If you cretaed email account for Fotobot on Google Mail leave this fields unchanged.<br>
<br>
That's all for fast start, go to main window and press START button, after that Fotobot will start to make photos.<br>
<br>
Control via SMS:<br>
1) password required in settings;<br>
2) launch Fotobot by pressing START (Fotobot checks for SMS only when started);<br>
3) send SMS to phone with started Fotobot:<br>
<br>
[SMS body]<br>
passwd pass <br>
update 300 <br>
network on <br>
log on <br>
jpg 50 <br>
status on <br>
<br>
Description:<br>
passwd - password <br>
update 300 - make photo each 300sec<br>
network on - photo to email and storage, network off - photo to storage<br>
log on - log will be attached to email<br>
log on 50 - log will be attached to email (size of log is 50Kb)<br>
flash on - photo with flash<br>
jpg 50 - JPEG quality<br>
status on - after that you will receive SMS with report.<br>
<br>
