<h2>User Manual</h2>

This simple, lightweight and free application will help you to monitor remote places. You will receive photos and videos in you email inbox. Designed for slow Internet connections. Email contains photo, video and detailed information about device (GSM network signal strength, battery charge and temperature, free space in device storage). Most of settings can be changed remotely via SMS.<br>
<br>
Below are main features of Fotobot:<br>
<br>
1) connect to Internet via Wi-Fi or MobileData;<br>
<br>
2) stays running (doesn't allow Android to kick out from memory);<br>
<br>
3) works on all devices starting from Android 2.3;<br>
<br>
4) installation package less than 1.9MB (important for old phones with small amount of internal storage);<br>
<br>
5) can be adjusted for slow-CPU devices (has an option for timing between processes).<br>
<br>
6) detailed reports via email (signal strength (db), battery charge(%), battery temperature (C), free space (MB));<br>
<br>
7) can be controlled via SMS;<br>
<br>
8) camera flash;<br>
<br>
9) internla/external(SD) storage;<br>
<br>
10) offline mode (just saving photos into storage);<br>
<br>
11) autofocus;<br>
<br>
12) video recording;<br>
<br>
13) upload video to FTP;<br>
<br>
14) voltage control (alert SMS when circuit off).<br>
